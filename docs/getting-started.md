# Getting Started

## 1. Open the bot

Start a chat with [@SaveAsVideoFreebot](https://t.me/SaveAsVideoFreebot) and press **Start**.

## 2. Send a video link

Copy the link to a video from a supported platform — YouTube, TikTok, Instagram or Twitter/X — and send it to the bot.

## 3. Get your video

The bot downloads the video and sends the file back to you directly in the chat.

## Tips

- YouTube videos and Shorts are downloaded in good quality where available.
- TikTok videos are downloaded quickly and without watermarks.
- Only publicly accessible videos can be downloaded.
